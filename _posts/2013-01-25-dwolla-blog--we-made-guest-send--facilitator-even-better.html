Last week, we were really proud to introduce our newest API endpoint – Guest Send.In short, Guest Send allowed you to send money from any bank account, to any person, in just one line of code.Over the past week, we’ve gotten a ton of feedback, and decided to fine tune that method.So today, we’re announcing two new features: Guest Send now has the ability to send money to any person – Dwolla user, or not.Simply set the ‘destinationType‘ parameter to Dwolla, Email, or Phone.
