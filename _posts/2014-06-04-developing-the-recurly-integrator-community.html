Channel Development is a key component of any effective revenue growth strategy. For solutions like Recurly that are highly self-service and strive to be developer friendly, the community of consultants and creative agencies that work with our platform are a growing source of new business. As the technology experts serving their clients, they are in uniquely positioned to promote Recurly. As a result, our Sales and Customer Success teams recently started an ongoing project to identify and track individuals that have worked with Recurly in an effort to align the objectives of the customer, integrator and Recurly team.Identification is our first step.
