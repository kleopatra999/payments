Facebook virtual payments (aka Facebook credits) are a special type of online currency that allows users to purchase games and applications within the world’s most popular social networking platform.After entering your credit card information once, you can easily buy, spend and even earn credits across more than 350 applications within Facebook.The program has been so successful that more than 70 percent of all virtual transactions on Facebook use these credits.What’s more, less than 2 percent of Facebook subscribers have begun using this virtual currency, suggesting almost unlimited growth potential for the payment platform.But what is driving this growth, and how are Facebook virtual payments helping to transform the world of online shopping?
