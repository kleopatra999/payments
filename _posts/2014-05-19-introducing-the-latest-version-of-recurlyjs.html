Since launching the prior version of Recurly.js, we’ve been listening closely to our customers and tracking feedback.A consistent theme we heard across our population was the limited ability to customize the checkout flow.Recurly’s latest version of Recurly.js provides you with the ease to securely accept payments and the flexibility to build a customized subscription checkout.
