Its no secret that Apple is an extremely influential player in the technology space, therefore its venture into payments with Apple Pay will most definitely have implications on mobile payments at large.But, is it the bridge the industry needs to finally create a mobile payments standard?While that will take time to determine, one thing is certain: Apple Pay will help drive innovation.SecureNet is committed to a seamless experience both online and offline, and this announcement gives us a new platform to further leverage our technology. At SecureNet we place a strong focus on customers and Apple Pay will provide another gateway to help our merchants connect with them.
