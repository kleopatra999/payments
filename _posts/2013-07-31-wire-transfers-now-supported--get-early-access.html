In keeping with tradition, we’re rolling out a new feature to a small handful of Dwolla members.Dwolla now supports wire transfers for deposits.In the event your business would like to drop $1 billion into your Dwolla account, now your business or government organization can do so.Initially, the feature is available for business accounts and is request only while we scale this feature with our financial partners and a limited group of companies.This feature is useful in the following scenarios: When a $50MM dollar transaction limit just isn’t enough, and your business or government organization needs to get more money into the network.
