Even payment giants like Visa get nervous about digital disruption in financial services.With the release of Visa Checkout, which replace’s the company’s V.me product, and Visa’s posh new Innovation Lab in San Francisco, it’s clear Visa is determined to be a player in digital payments.The company is also looking to work with partners to create the best services possible for customers.  Visa has been about developing relationships with partners since its earliest days, said Brad Greene, VP of digital solutions in developed markets.
