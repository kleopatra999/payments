MasterCard celebrated 40 years in Canada yesterday by announcing the corporation’s partnership with MaRS Discovery District to open a new networking space for entrepreneurs and announcing MasterCard N>XT, the first payment network developer challenge with Rogers and CIBC as co-sponsors, to be held at the MaRS Center.“Innovation is in our DNA,” said Betty DeVita, President of MasterCard Canada.“Over the last 40 years, MasterCard has led the way in bringing payment innovations to the fingertips of Canadians and merchants – from chip & PIN to Tap & Go – we continue to find ways to make electronic payments a seamless, convenient and rewarding choice for Canadians.” The 2-day challenge will take place September 28th and 29th at MaRS and will search for Canada’s next great developer and breakthrough payment solution.With access to MasterCard’s commerce infrastructure, professional and student application developers are invited to create their own payment applications and mobile solutions.
