It’s a well known fact that news organizations over the world are discussing when and how they should start using a paywall, if they already haven’t.Certainly inevitable if you look at the trends in newspaper industry, where revenue from readers is starting to be higher than the from ads. But there are lot’s of different ways to go, from a rigid wall to an open approach where all material is available with the possibility for readers to pay for what they like.A global payment solution It’s becoming quite a hassle to read news online.All news sites are implementing their own walls and subscription plans.
