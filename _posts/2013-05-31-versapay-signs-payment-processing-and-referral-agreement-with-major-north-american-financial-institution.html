Toronto, ON – May 31, 2013 – VersaPay Corporation (TSXV: VPY) (“VersaPay”),  a leading provider of cloud-based financial technology solutions, today announced that it has entered into a payment processing and referral agreement with TD Merchant Services, an operating division of The Toronto-Dominion Bank (“TD”).Under the terms of the agreement, VersaPay and TD will refer eligible business-to-business merchant customers to each other in order to take advantage of VersaPay’s cloud-based payment processing solution and TD’s credit card and debit card processing services, respectively. “We are excited to establish this business relationship with one of North America’s pre-eminent financial services organizations,” commented Bill McGill, CEO of VersaPay.Mr.McGill continued, “We believe that this collaboration will significantly broaden the market for our unique cloud-based electronic invoice presentment and payment platform.
